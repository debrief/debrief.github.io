Obtaining Debrief Support

Debrief is a capable, fast, intuitive and immensely powerful tool for maritime analysis. Indeed, as well as being used by many navies around the world, it’s also used by governmental and non-governmental agencies in many countries. Being open-source software, it is free to use; it works on multiple platforms; is well-documented with written tutorials, video training, and a comprehensive user guide; and, has been under continuous maintenance for 20-years. It truly is a great tool.

As it is open source software, we are committed to providing a level of free support for all users of Debrief (and have no plans to do otherwise). However, please understand that though this support is  given freely, we have to give priority to our support contracts.

Support Levels

We offer 3 levels of support for organisations for whom rapid response and issue resolution is key. These are Support Basic, Support Plus, and Custom support. We’ll look at each level of support in turn.

Free

This level of support is open to all users. As mentioned, Debrief if hosted on GitHub and support issues can be submitted in one of two ways by both registered and unregistered users.

  • Registered users can open an Issue on our GitHub repository (Debrief on GitHub). Once submitted, the user will then be notified of any updates to their issue in GitHub. If you read the previous post (How to Access the Debrief GitHub Repository) you’ll know what benefits this offers to serious businesses.

Registered users can do so by clicking on the New Issue button in the repository and filling in details of the message (click here for details):

  • Unregistered users can email us at support@debrief.info. Be aware that, unfortunately, we are unable to provide updates on issues submitted directly via email.

Structure for Submitting Issues

If you do need to submit an issue, we ask all users to use the same reporting structure when submitting issues. If you use a desktop email client, such as Outlook, then click here; if you don't, then click here.

Access to Free Tutorials

All users have access to the free tutorials (video and pdf), user guide (pdf and online), and case studies.

Support Basic

Our first level of paid support is called Support Basic. Despite its lowly name, Support Basic provides ongoing support which allows analysts to remain responsive to any dynamic challenges in Debrief. Suitable for smaller organisations with just a single Debrief analyst, this level offers:

  • Telephone Support
  • Assisted Issue Recording
  • New releases by Post (including MSI)

Click here for further details

Support Plus

The next level of paid support is geared towards larger organisations and those with more than one analyst. Support Plus offers the following services:

  • Onsite support
  • Assisted Issue Recording
  • New releases by Post (including MSI)
  • Printed user guide each user
  • Annual check-up
  • Accelerated bug fixes
  • New features and enhancements
  • Product extensions

Click here for further details

Custom Support

As open source software, you can customise Debrief as you wish and really make it work for you. However, if you’d prefer not to do that in-house, or you would like the reassurance of having our experts support you, help develop new capabilities, or even leverage your analyst’s contextual knowledge then we can assist via either a fixed price or a bespoke package.

Fixed Price

With our fixed price package, we can develop new components and capabilities such as customised symbol libraries, and analysis tool capabilities to evolve alongside your analysis requirements, which allows your analysts to focus on delivering effective results.

For example, we can create symbols to represent your own particular platforms and vehicles; can create new file import and export interfaces; and, can widen support for complex file types in Debrief.

Click here for further details

Bespoke

If you require something that’s not listed above, or you have specific requirements such as custom development projects then please get in touch (click on the contact us link below).

Examples of where we can assist you are in development projects aimed at making better use of your analyst’s effort, and improving results through innovative analysis of data types, new concepts, and access to newer and better performance metrics.

Whatever your requirements - even if it’s only to obtain an understanding of what is possible with Debrief - click here to contact us for a free, no-obligation discussion.
